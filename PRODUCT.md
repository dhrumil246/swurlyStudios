# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
General consumers exploring our offerings.

## Product Purpose
Establish brand identity and trust by showcasing our offerings.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Modern, Innovative. Voice should be confident and clear.

## Anti-references
Avoid cluttered, generic, or overly technical interfaces. Avoid standard template layouts; lean toward an Apple-like aesthetic with minimalism, large typography, and high-quality imagery.

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell:** Rely on high-quality imagery and bold layouts to convey value.
- **Practice restraint:** Embrace minimalism and negative space to let content breathe.
- **Expert confidence:** Use large, deliberate typography that speaks with authority and clarity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (WCAG AA).
